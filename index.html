Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about tax filing with TaxReturn.

What is the last date for ITR filing for FY 2024-25?

The Income Tax Department has extended the ITR filing due date for FY 2024-25 (AY 2025-26) to September 16, 2025, for individuals not subject to audit.

Who is required to file an ITR for FY 2024-25?

Anyone with an annual income exceeding ₹2.5 lakh must file an ITR. For senior citizens (60-79 years), the limit is ₹3 lakh, and for super senior citizens (80+), it's ₹5 lakh.

How secure is my data on TaxReturn?

TaxReturn uses 256-bit SSL encryption and follows strict data protection protocols. Your information is stored securely and never shared with third parties without consent.

Can I get assisted tax filing services?

Yes! TaxReturn provides expert-assisted filing where experienced CAs and tax professionals guide you through the entire process, ensuring accuracy and maximum savings.

What if I receive a tax notice after filing?

TaxReturn provides free notice management for returns filed through our platform. Our tax experts help you respond to any notices promptly and effectively.
